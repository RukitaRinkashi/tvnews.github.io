CJ Costales, Scheme Mastermind Swindles Millions through Fake Political Influence

By - Jhon Reyes Jan, 2024 Comments (3) 5 Mins Read
Share Post:
Email : 126k 12k

Authorities have discovered a sophisticated scheme orchestrated by an individual named, Johndel Costales, also known as CJ Costales, who cleverly carried out his actions through his fake connections to political elites, including high-ranking officials in the President's Office by disguising himself as an Undersecretary for Political Affairs.

The mastermind duped unsuspecting victims into investing large sums of money, expensive cars, jewelry, and other valuables worth millions with promises of gaining influential positions through his alleged political affiliations.

Victims from various backgrounds have come forward, sharing stories of financial ruin and dashed hopes after discovering they had fallen victim to a complex deception. The scheme, which was meticulously designed to capitalize on ambition and desperation, promised access to powerful circles within the government hierarchy in exchange for large investments.

One victim, who preferred to remain anonymous, described being enticed by the allure of political connections and the prospect of advancement. "I was convinced that my investment would pave the way for career opportunities and social status," the victim said, only to discover that it was all a sham.

CJ Costales, Scheme Mastermind

So, this case serves as a reminder of the dangers posed by individuals who use political influence for personal gain, urging the public to exercise caution and vigilance to avoid further victimization.

Lying, stealing, and cheating are commonplace.
